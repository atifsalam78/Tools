Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our image compression tool

Is this service really free?

Yes! ImageCompressPro is completely free to use with no hidden charges. We support the service through non-intrusive advertisements.

Are my images secure?

Absolutely. All processing happens in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can compress to these formats or keep the original format.

Is there a file size limit?

There's no strict limit, but very large images (over 20MB) may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities.
